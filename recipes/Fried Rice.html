Chicken Fried Rice

How to Make Chicken Fried Rice

Looking for an easy plate of Homemade Chicken Fried Rice that’s brimming with flavor and can be customized to your taste? Then this is the recipe for you!

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

You'll only need five ingredients to make these irresistible Chicken Fried Rice:

  • Cooked Rice: About 2 cups of cooked and cooled white rice (leftover rice works best).
  • Cooked Chicken: About 1 cup of cooked and diced chicken (you can use leftover cooked chicken or cook chicken specifically for this dish).
  • Vegetables: A mix of chopped vegetables such as carrots, peas, bell peppers, and onions. You'll need about 1 cup of mixed vegetables.
  • Eggs: 2 eggs, lightly beaten.
  • Soy Sauce: Around 2-3 tablespoons of soy sauce for flavor and color.

Recipe instructions (taken from the book)

  1. Sauté Chicken & Veggies: Stir-fry diced cooked chicken, mixed chopped vegetables, garlic, and ginger in oil.
  2. Push Aside & Scramble Eggs: Push mixture aside, scramble eggs in the pan.
  3. Add Rice: Mix in cooked and cooled rice, breaking up clumps.
  4. Season & Flavor: Drizzle with soy sauce, a touch of sesame oil, and optional spices.
  5. Garnish & Serve: Top with chopped scallions, serve hot for a tasty Chicken Fried Rice.

We have determined the nutritional value of oil for frying based on a retention value of 10% after cooking. Amount will vary depending on cooking time and temperature, ingredient density, and specific type of oil used.
